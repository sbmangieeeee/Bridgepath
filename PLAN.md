# Delivery Plan and Review Gates

## Gate 0 — Governance

Restore browser walkthrough capability; obtain official Standard 3 objectives; appoint curriculum, cultural, child-safety/privacy, illustration, and audio reviewers; approve adult verification; complete data inventory, threat model, retention, consent, processor review, and pilot protocol.

**Pass:** signed curriculum mapping, research review, legal feasibility, and locked slice.

## Gate 1 — Experience validation

Test the implemented low-fidelity flow and complete Market Day script with 5–8 children and several parents under approved research consent.

**Pass:** at least 80% complete core tasks without facilitator instruction; observed misunderstandings are resolved.

## Gate 2 — Production foundations

Replace local adapters with parent auth, adult verification, Supabase persistence/RLS, server-derived evidence, recovery, and privacy operations. Add authorization and contract tests first.

**Pass:** threat-model actions closed, keyboard-complete accounts, no cross-family access.

## Gate 3 — Content and game production

Commission reviewed art/audio and finalize the six-stop learning loop, idempotent evidence, resume and parent projections.

**Pass:** correct, incorrect, interrupted, remediated and completed paths reconcile.

## Gate 4 — Hardening

Test supported phones, tablets and laptops; constrained networks; accessibility; loading/empty/error states; backup restore; export/deletion; support and incident drills.

**Pass:** no critical/high security or accessibility defects and no uncaught console errors.

## Gate 5 — Controlled pilot

Release in cohorts, collect necessary learning/product events only, provide support, and review misconceptions, completion, hint use, return behaviour and qualitative feedback.

**Pass:** parent reports match child evidence and educator review confirms intended learning.

## Browser milestone checklist

Test every visible control; parent and child roles; correct/incorrect/repeated errors; pause/refresh/resume; audio and failure fallback; mobile/tablet/desktop; 200% zoom; reduced motion; keyboard; loading/empty/errors; console/network; and baseline screenshots. Compilation alone never passes a gate.

## External blockers

Production pilot activation is blocked until named reviewers, pilot jurisdiction, verification provider, budget/team, success thresholds, legal advice, and research consent are confirmed.
